Policies & Guidelines

We believe in transparency so you know exactly what to expect when booking with us.

Booking Windows

We kindly ask that all visits be booked at least 48 hours in advance to guarantee availability. Holiday periods may require earlier booking due to high demand.

Cancellations & Refunds

  • More than 24 hours before scheduled visit: Full refund or credit.
  • Within 24 hours: 50% fee applied.
  • No-shows: Full payment due.

We understand emergencies happen and will do our best to be flexible.

Payment Due Dates

Payment is due at the time of booking unless otherwise agreed. We accept Venmo, Zelle, and credit card payments.

Veterinary / Emergency Authorization

By booking with us, you authorize us to seek veterinary care for your pet in case of emergency. We will always attempt to contact you first, but your pet’s well-being comes first. All veterinary expenses are the responsibility of the pet owner.

Household Tasks

We’re happy to take care of light household tasks during visits, such as:

  • Bringing in mail or packages
  • Watering small plants
  • Taking trash bins to the curb

Please note: Tasks should take less than 10 minutes. Larger requests may require additional fees.
